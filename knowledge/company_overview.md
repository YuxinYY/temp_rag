# Benjamin Moore & Co. — Company & Sales Structure

## Company Overview

Benjamin Moore & Co. is a premium American paint and coatings manufacturer founded in 1883, headquartered in Parsippany, New Jersey. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway. Benjamin Moore manufactures and distributes interior and exterior paints, stains, primers, and specialty coatings under several brand families including Benjamin Moore, Coronado, INSL-X, and C+K (California Products).

Benjamin Moore products are sold exclusively through independent retailers — they are not available in big-box stores like Home Depot or Lowe's. This independent dealer model is central to the company's premium brand positioning.

## Sales Organization

### Territory Structure
Sales are organized by **Territory**, which represents an individual sales representative or account manager. Each territory is typically named after the rep who owns it (e.g., "ADAM NAGLE - ACE ONLY", "ALEX SOUTELO"). There are approximately 245 active territories across North America and international markets.

- Territories marked **"ACE ONLY"** indicate reps whose accounts are exclusively Ace Hardware dealers — a major retail partner that carries a private-label line of Benjamin Moore products under the ACE brand.
- Some territories are designated **"PORTAL ACCOUNT"** or **"INTERNAL CUSTOMER"**, representing company-owned stores, internal transfers, or administrative accounts rather than true external sales.

## Geographic Coverage

### Domestic (USA)
The United States represents the majority of sales volume. Key states include Florida, New York, California, Texas, and New England states — historically Benjamin Moore's strongest markets.

### Canada
Canada is a significant secondary market, particularly in Ontario, Quebec, and British Columbia. Canadian pricing is in CAD and subject to separate promotional programs.

### International
Benjamin Moore operates in select international markets through distributor relationships:
- **Caribbean**: Bermuda, Bahamas, Barbados, Jamaica, Puerto Rico, Trinidad & Tobago, Dominican Republic, and several island territories
- **Europe**: United Kingdom, Ireland, France, Spain, Italy, Greece, Netherlands, Poland, and others
- **Asia-Pacific**: Australia, Japan, South Korea, Singapore, Philippines, Thailand, Taiwan, Vietnam, China
- **Latin America**: Mexico, Colombia, Chile, Guatemala, Honduras, Panama
- **Middle East**: United Arab Emirates, Israel
- **Other**: Guam, Uzbekistan

International volumes are generally lower per territory but important for brand development.
